Wuhan Lockdown
Jan 23th, Wuhan, China

Lock down policy effective in Wuhan. China started to build multiple mobile cabin hospitals. The daily testing ability in Wuhan at this point is only 200.

global public-health emergency
Jan 30th, WHO

WHO declares a global public-health emergency on Jan 30th.

mobile cabin hospitals & Diamond Princess

Major mobile cabin hospitals started to take patients in China. Meanwhile in Japan, it is confirmed that collective infection of Covid-19 occurred in the cruise "Diamond Princess", which leads 10 confirmed cases.

Named COVID-19

On 11 February 2020, WHO announces that the new coronavirus disease will be called "COVID-19." Coronavirus cases start to spike in South Korea. Iran outbreak begins from around Feb 19th.

Superspreader

The Japanese cruise "Diamond Princess" has 542 confirmed cases. 'Superspreader' in South Korea infects nearly 40 people with coronavirus in the Shincheonji Church of Jesus.

Italy outbreak

Italy outbreak begins. Small towns hit by the outbreak are placed under quarantine.

pandemic

Italy placed all 60 million residents on lockdown. WHO declared the outbreak a pandemic on Mar 11th.

A US national emergency is declared over the novel coronavirus outbreak on Mar 13th.

COVID-19 spread in Europe

By March 2020, all EU member states had reported COVID-19 cases. The number of cases has since continued to increase.

On Mar 19th, China reported no new locally spread infections for the first time since the pandemic began.

Outbreak in NY

New York City confirms 21,000 cases, making it the biggest epicenter of the outbreak in the US.

On Mar 26th, total confirmed cases in the US reach 82,404 — the highest in the world — surpassing China's 81,782 and Italy's 80,589.
